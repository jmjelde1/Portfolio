Backcountry Safety Information

Backcountry skiing involves a higher risk than is associated with skiing in-bounds at resorts. The goal of this research study is to evaluate where our human biases lie when it comes to perceiving the angles of snowy slopes in the backcountry, in hopes that one day we may be able to re-work current avalanche safety training to train skiers and snowboarders more effectively.
