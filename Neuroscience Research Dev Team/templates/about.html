Russ Costa

The principal investigator for this project is Dr. Russell E. Costa, an Associate Professor of Honors & Neuroscience at Westminster College in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Dr. Costa is a cognitive scientist interested in human perception and attention in high-risk environments. As a backcountry skier and mountaineer, he is interested in how perceptions of slope angle influence decisions about mountain travel in avalanche terrain. If you have any questions about this project or his research more broadly, you may contact Dr. Costa via email at rcosta@westminstercollege.edu.
